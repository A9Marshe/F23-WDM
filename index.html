Comparison between the classifiers

It is evident that the Decision tree classifier is the most accurate:

Decision Tree:   76%

Logistic Regression:   45%

Naive Bayse:   06%

In general, decision trees are better than logistic regression when there are large numbers of features with less data-sets (with low noise), while logistic regression is better than decision trees when there are categorical independent variables. Naive Bayes' independence assumption is a crucial factor for the classifier's. It seems that Naive Bayse assumption is not met with our dataset while the Decision Tree classifier was able to figure out some complex relationships between the entries in the dataset.
